Our Story

Designed in Belgrade.
Made to last.

LOOQ started in 2019 in a small Dorćol workshop with one idea: eyewear shouldn't cost a fortune or cost the planet.

Every frame is crafted from plant-based acetate and recycled titanium. We cut out the middlemen so you get premium quality at honest prices.
