Our Story

Three generations of flour-dusted hands.

What started as Oma's weekend hobby in 1998 has grown into Novi Sad's most beloved bakery. We still use her original sourdough starter — now 26 years old.

Every loaf is shaped by hand. Every pastry is made from scratch. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromises.

1998
Oma opens a tiny bread stall at the Saturday market
2005
First brick-and-mortar shop on Zmaj Jovina
2018
Won "Best Artisan Bakery" at Serbian Food Awards
2024
Expanded with a second location and coffee bar
